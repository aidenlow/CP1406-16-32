About Us

The Barrier Reef Orchestra is a product of North Queensland Ensembles Inc.
North Queensland Ensembles Inc. is an Incorporated Association registered in the State of Queensland. It is also a tax deductible fund listed on the Register of Cultural Organizations under Subdivision 30-B of the Income tax Assessment Act 1997. It is based in Townsville, North Queensland, Australia.
